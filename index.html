About us

Who we are?

Team "SABDA" is a group of young college enthusiasts who aim to learn and gather as much experience and skills as possible and apply them in real world to do our part in taking the society a step further. We are the 1st year CSE students of VIT presenting you this web app that will help the one that have helped us reach here, "THE PARENTS"!!!

Our Vision

With this kiddie app, we aim to act as an extra hand to raise your children for the busy working parents and be a friend to the children in this new covid world where everyone are isolated from one another. WE aim to bring various features that will make help in the growth of the children and a platform parents can have faith upon to in assisting their children.
